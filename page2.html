Hall 3 Facilities

The two main facilities that can be used by residents are the Comms Hall and the Gym

The Comms Hall is located at Level 2 above the office and the Gym is located at Level 1



To get to the gym, walk through the Hall Office and it will be to the right.





To get to the comms hall, walk up the staircase on either side of the Office.
