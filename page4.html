Gymnasium

No pain, No Gain!



If I am not mistaken, not every hall has a gym, and as Hall 3 is reknowned as a Sports Hall, it is useful to have a gym for the sportsmen to build strength.

The gym is relatively small, only allowing up to 4 pax at one time.

That being said, there should be enough equipment to get in a decent workout.





There is a rack that can be adjusted for either squats or bench press.

There is are several treadmills, a rowing machine, weighing scale, cable machines, free weights and a few benches.





The gym is open from 9am to 8pm everyday.
