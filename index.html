NTU Hall 3



To get to Hall 3 by public transport, the fastest way is to take the MRT to Pioneer (East-West Line)

Bus Stop Exit B and take bus 179 and alight at Lee Wee Nam Library. It is then a 5 minute walk.

However, there are also shuttle buses that go to other MRT stations but at scheduled times and only for NTU students and staff.
